## Executive Summary

ezmate.ai is an AI agency serving Australian and English-speaking SMBs with AI Agents (chatbots/voice), AI automation, and AI consulting services. We target **70%+ profit margins** through a **fully automated marketing funnel** that generates consistent leads and sales.

**Key Differentiator**: Custom solutions priced by value delivered, not fixed packages. Every client receives a tailored proposal based on their specific pain points and business scale.

## 9. Sales Call Framework ("Sell Like a Doctor")

### 8.1 AI Audit Framework

```
1. DIAGNOSE (60% of call)
   ├── "Tell me about your business"
   ├── "What's your biggest challenge right now?"
   ├── "How is this affecting your revenue?"
   └── Dan Sullivan Question: "If we were talking 12 months
       from now, what would have to happen for you to be
       happy with your AI investment?"

2. PRESCRIBE (30% of call)
   ├── Summarize their pain points
   ├── Present tailored solution
   └── Show relevant case study

3. CLOSE (10% of call)
   ├── Present pricing with Godfather Offer
   ├── Handle objections
   └── Next steps / Payment
```

### 8.2 Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "What's the cost of NOT having AI? How many leads are you losing?" |
| "Need to think" | "What specifically do you need to think about? Let's address it now." |
| "Bad timing" | "When would be perfect? Let's lock in today's price for that date." |
| "Tried AI before" | "What went wrong? Our 7-day guarantee means you see results fast." |
